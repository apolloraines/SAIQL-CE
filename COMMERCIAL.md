# Commercial Licensing for SAIQL

## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

You need a commercial license if:

1. **Revenue Threshold**
   - Your organization exceeds $10M USD in gross annual revenue

2. **Hosted Service**
   - You offer SAIQL as a database-as-a-service (DBaaS)
   - You provide managed SAIQL instances to third parties
   - Regardless of your revenue level

3. **Corporate Structure**
   - You're majority-owned by a company exceeding $10M revenue
   - You're operating on behalf of a larger parent organization

---

## Grace Period ($6M - $10M)

If your revenue crosses $6M:

1. **Notify us within 30 days**
   - Email: apollo@saiql.ai
   - Include: Company name, estimated revenue, how you're using SAIQL

2. **12-month grace period**
   - Continue using SAIQL during good-faith licensing discussions
   - Work with us to find a fair licensing arrangement

3. **Why?**
   - We understand growth takes time
   - Licensing shouldn't disrupt your business
   - We want to support your success

---
